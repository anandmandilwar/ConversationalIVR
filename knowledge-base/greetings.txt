# Metro Parking Authority - Greeting and Introduction

## Welcome Message
Welcome to Metro Parking Authority's automated payment and information system. We manage parking violations for the City of Metro and surrounding areas.

## Available Services
As a Metro Parking Authority customer, you can:
- Check your current balance and violation details
- Make a payment on one or more violations
- Submit a dispute for a violation you believe was issued in error
- Check the status of an existing dispute
- Request information about payment plans

## Business Hours
- Monday through Friday: 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern
- Saturday: 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM Eastern
- Sunday: Closed

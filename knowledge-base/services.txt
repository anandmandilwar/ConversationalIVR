# Metro Parking Authority - Available Services

## Balance Inquiry
Customers can check their balance by providing:
- License plate number AND state of registration
- OR Citation number (found on the violation notice)

The system will display:
- Total amount due
- Number of outstanding violations
- Individual violation details including date, location, and amount

## Payment Processing
Metro Parking Authority accepts:
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- Electronic checks (ACH)

Payment options:
- Pay all violations at once
- Select individual violations to pay
- Minimum payment: $1.00
- Maximum IVR payment: $2,500.00 (larger amounts must be paid online or in person)

### Payment Fees
- Credit/Debit card: 2.5% convenience fee (minimum $1.50)
- Electronic check: $1.00 flat fee

## Dispute Submission
Customers may dispute a violation for the following reasons:
- Vehicle was sold before violation date
- License plate was stolen or lost
- Meter malfunction
- Signage not visible
- Wrong vehicle cited
- Already paid

Required information for disputes:
- Citation number
- Reason for dispute
- Supporting documentation description (to be mailed or uploaded online)

Dispute processing time: 10-15 business days

## Payment Plans
Payment plans are available for balances over $200:
- Minimum 3 monthly payments
- Maximum 12 monthly payments
- $25 setup fee
- Automatic payment required
- Payment plans must be set up by speaking with an agent

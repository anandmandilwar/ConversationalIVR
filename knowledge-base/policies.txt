# Metro Parking Authority - Policies and Procedures

## Authentication Policy
Customers must verify their identity before accessing account information:

### Primary Authentication (License Plate)
- License plate number (letters and numbers)
- State of vehicle registration

### Alternative Authentication (Citation)
- Citation number (10-digit number starting with "MP")

### Failed Authentication
- After 3 failed attempts, customer should be transferred to an agent
- Do not reveal whether an account exists for security purposes

## Payment Policies

### Accepted Payment Methods
- Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover
- Debit cards with Visa/MC logo
- Electronic checks (routing and account number)

### Payment Restrictions
- Maximum IVR payment: $2,500.00
- Accounts with more than 10 violations must speak with an agent
- Accounts in bankruptcy cannot make payments through IVR
- Accounts in legal dispute cannot make payments through IVR

### Refund Policy
- Duplicate payments: Full refund within 5-7 business days
- Successful dispute: Full refund within 10-15 business days
- Customer error: No refunds for payments made in error

## Dispute Policies

### Eligible Dispute Reasons
1. Vehicle sold prior to violation
2. Plates stolen/lost (police report required)
3. Meter malfunction
4. Signage issue
5. Wrong vehicle cited
6. Already paid (proof required)
7. Rental vehicle (rental agreement required)

### Ineligible Dispute Reasons
- Forgot to pay meter
- Didn't see the sign
- Was only parked for a minute
- Someone else was driving
- Financial hardship (payment plan available instead)

## Transfer Policies

### Transfer to Agent When:
- Customer requests agent 3 times
- Account has more than 10 violations
- Account is in collections
- Account is in bankruptcy
- Payment plan request
- Complex dispute
- Customer is frustrated or upset

### After Hours
- Inform customer of business hours
- Offer self-service options
- Provide website and email contact
- Do not offer callback scheduling
